What We Do

Our Services

Full-scope commercial cleaning and maintenance — handled in-house, by our crew.

Commercial Cleaning

Scheduled cleaning programs for offices, retail spaces, and commercial facilities. Consistent crews, consistent results — no rotating strangers walking through your building.

Floor Care & Restoration

Strip, seal, wax, and buff hard floors. Carpet extraction and deep cleaning. We restore floors that look like they've been written off and keep maintained floors looking sharp.

Post-Construction Cleanup

Rough clean, final clean, and punch-list clean for new construction and renovation projects. We work with GCs and project managers who need a crew that understands job site timelines.

Facility Maintenance

Ongoing maintenance programs covering common areas, restrooms, breakrooms, and building exteriors. One point of contact for the work that keeps your facility running clean.

Window & Exterior

Interior and exterior window cleaning, pressure washing, and building facade maintenance. We handle the work that most cleaning companies quietly skip over.

Janitorial Programs

Custom recurring janitorial contracts for multi-tenant buildings, medical offices, schools, and industrial facilities. Scoped to your building, staffed by our people.

The Operation

Built on the Work, Not the Pitch

Cesar Salas has spent his career in the service industry — working through nearly every role it has to offer, from the ground floor up. That breadth of experience gave him something most operators don't have: a real understanding of what it takes to do the work right, and what it looks like when someone cuts corners. He started Vanguard Commercial because he wanted to build something honest — a company that creates real work opportunities for people who take pride in what they do.

Cesar's entrepreneurial drive isn't about growth for its own sake. It's about building a crew worth trusting, doing work worth standing behind, and creating a place where hard, honest effort is recognized and rewarded. Reliability and integrity aren't talking points at Vanguard — they're the reason clients call back.

Vanguard Commercial is based in Milwaukee, WI and serves the greater SE Wisconsin region — from Oconomowoc to the west, Oshkosh to the north, and Racine/Kenosha to the south. When Cesar isn't running the operation, you'll likely find him with his dog — a lively companion that matches his energy and keeps things real.
