ABOUT VANGUARD

Marcus Reeves, Founder

Marcus Reeves

Founder & Owner

I started Vanguard Commercial in 2014 after spending a decade in the cleaning industry watching the same problems repeat: companies that over-promise and under-deliver, crews that never look the same twice, and managers who never set foot on the job.

We built this company on a simple premise: show up when we say we will, do the work right, and charge a fair price. No contracts that lock you in, no subcontractors passing the buck. Just a crew that treats your facility like it's our own.
