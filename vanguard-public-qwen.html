About Vanguard

I started Vanguard because I was tired of watching good businesses get bad service.

After fifteen years running facilities for construction firms and property management companies, I saw the same pattern: cleaning crews that no-showed, subcontractors who didn't care, and promises that evaporated after the contract was signed.

Vanguard is built different. We're not a franchise. We're not a app-based gig crew. We're a real operation with employees who show up on time, do the work right, and answer when you call. Every job gets a dedicated crew that knows your building and your standards.

This company exists to serve businesses that take pride in their space. If that's you, we should talk.
