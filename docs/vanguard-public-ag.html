OUR SERVICES

Commercial Cleaning

Comprehensive daily and weekly cleaning tailored to your operation's precise schedule. We maintain a pristine environment that reflects the professionalism of your business.

Floor Care & Restoration

Stripping, waxing, buffing, and intensive deep extraction for all hard surfaces and commercial carpets. Extend the life of your facility’s flooring investments.

Post-Construction Cleanup

Rapid, meticulous debris removal and phase cleaning after major builds or remodels. We turn a raw construction site into an operation-ready commercial space.

Facility Maintenance

Ongoing operational upkeep spanning minor repairs, lighting changes, and preventative physical maintenance. Keep everything functioning without hiring full-time staff.

Window & Exterior

Exterior pressure washing and streamback commercial window washing up to five stories. First impressions matter, and we guarantee the exterior reflects the standard inside.

Janitorial Programs

Restocking, sanitization runs, and mid-day porter services designed around high-traffic facilities. We silently support your heavy footfall without interrupting workflow.

THE CREW YOU RELY ON

Cesar Salas — Operations Director & Founder

Vanguard Commercial was built on a long history of hard work. After years in the service industry across many roles, Cesar Salas took his entrepreneurial spirit and channeled it into Vanguard. He didn't just want to build a business—he wanted to help others by creating genuine work opportunities, fostering growth, and honoring the value of hard, honest work.

Honesty isn't a talking point for us; it is a core value. When we operate your facility, you get a crew that is fundamentally reliable and trustworthy. We stand by our commitments, ensuring that every operation runs smoothly without excuses.

Known for his lively spirit and being a devoted dog lover, Cesar leads by example, bringing energy and integrity to every job. We are a working-class operation that brings polish to the discipline of maintenance. When Vanguard is locked in, the work is already done.
