What We Do

Full-service cleaning and maintenance for commercial properties that demand consistency.

Commercial Cleaning

Scheduled cleaning programs for offices, retail, medical facilities, and industrial spaces. Delivered on time, every time, by the same crew.

Floor Care & Restoration

Stripping, waxing, buffing, and deep-clean restoration for VCT, hardwood, tile, and polished concrete. We bring floors back from the dead.

Post-Construction Cleanup

Rough clean, final clean, and punch-list detail work for general contractors and developers. We work to your schedule and your GC's standards.

Facility Maintenance

Ongoing property upkeep that keeps facilities code-compliant and presentable. From restroom sanitation to common area management and minor repairs.

Window & Exterior

Interior and exterior window cleaning, pressure washing, and facade maintenance for storefronts, office buildings, and industrial properties.

Janitorial Programs

Custom recurring janitorial contracts for buildings that need daily, weekly, or rotating service. We build the program around your operation, not ours.

The Operation

Built From
The Floor Up

Cesar Salas has spent his career working just about every role the service industry has to offer — from bussing tables and running kitchens to managing facilities, coordinating crews, and building operations from the ground up. He knows what it means to work hard for someone else, and he never forgot it. That history is in the DNA of Vanguard Commercial: a business built by someone who respects the work and the people doing it.

When Cesar launched Vanguard, he wasn't just trying to start a company — he was trying to build something that gives people a real shot. Steady work, fair wages, a team that takes pride in what they do and grows with the business. He believes honest, hard work is one of the most dignified things a person can offer, and he's built Vanguard around that conviction. Honesty isn't a value he hangs on a wall — it's how he operates, every day, with every client and every crew member. No corners cut. No excuses made.

Ask anyone who's worked with Cesar and they'll say the same thing: he does what he says, he shows up when it counts, and you always know where you stand. That reliability is what Vanguard promises every client from Milwaukee to Oconomowoc, Oshkosh to Racine. When he's not running the operation, you'll find him with his dogs — spirited, loyal, and a good reminder that the best qualities in a crew aren't that different from the best qualities in a good dog.
